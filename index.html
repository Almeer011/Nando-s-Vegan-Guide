Nando’s Vegan & Vegetarian Options UK

Nando’s UK offers a growing variety of plant-based meals. All vegan and vegetarian items are clearly labeled on the menu. The restaurant uses pea protein and vegetable-based patties to meet dietary preferences. Cross-contact is possible due to shared cooking equipment.

Full menu breakdowns, including allergen filters and regional options, are also curated at this independent UK-based menu reference source.

The Great Imitator – A Pea Protein-Based Alternative

The Great Imitator Wrap mimics the taste and texture of Nando’s classic chicken using pea protein strips. It’s grilled, not fried. Ask for no mayo or cheese to ensure full vegan compliance.

Nutritional Value: 599 kcal, 25.9g protein, 66.1g carbs
(Source: Nando’s Nutritional Guide 2024)

Spiced Chickpea Burger – Vegan When Modified

Made from chickpeas, sweetcorn, and spinach, the Spiced Chickpea Burger can be served without mayo to make it vegan.

Contains: Wheat, soy
Suitable for: Vegetarians, modified vegans

The Beanie – A Classic Vegetarian Option

The Beanie Patty includes sweetcorn, lentils, peas, and red pepper. It’s served in a burger or wrap. Contains egg and milk.

Nutritional Value: 562 kcal, 20g protein
(Source: Nando’s UK Allergen Guide)

Available Vegan Sides at Nando’s UK

  • Peri-Salted Chips
  • Spicy Rice
  • Corn on the Cob (no butter)
  • Long Stem Broccoli
  • Garlic Bread (check for butter content)

Always verify the allergen matrix before ordering.

Customizing Your Vegan Meal at Nando’s

  • Remove dairy sauces like Perinaise or garlic mayo
  • Choose Plain, Lemon & Herb, or Medium Peri-Peri
  • Add toppings like avocado, grilled mushrooms, or vegan cheese (if available)

Cooking Practices and Cross-Contamination Risks

Cross-contamination may occur due to shared grills and fryers. Nando’s does not guarantee an allergen-free environment.

(Source: Nando’s UK FAQs, 2024)

No Vegan Desserts on the Menu

No desserts at Nando’s UK are suitable for vegans. Most contain milk, eggs, or butter.

Beverages That Are Safe for Vegans

  • Coca-Cola (Regular and Diet)
  • Sprite Zero
  • Rubicon Mango

Check with staff about alcoholic drinks due to animal-derived filters.
